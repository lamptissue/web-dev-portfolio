About

Hello!

My name is Neil Carribine, and I am a full stack developer based in Folkestone, UK. I specialised in Javascript, and have a background in illustration and animation which helps me bring a creative and playful approach to my designs. I use tools like Figma and the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign) to bring my ideas to life. I believe that good design is not only functional, but also visually appealing, and I strive to create work that meets both of these criteria.

In my free time, I enjoy staying active through activities like cycling, snowboarding, and walking by the beach. I believe that a healthy balance between work and personal interests is key to happiness and productivity.
